What is Ukraine?

Ukraine is a country in Eastern Europe. It is the second-largest country by area in Europe after Russia, which it borders to the east and north-east.
Ukraine also shares borders with Belarus, Poland, Slovakia, Hungary, Romania and Moldova; and has a coastline along the Sea of Azov and the Black Sea.
It spans an area of 603,628 km2, with a population of 41.3 million, and is the eighth-most populous country in Europe.
The nation's capital and largest city is Kyiv.
